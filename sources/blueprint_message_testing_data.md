## 1. Message to Democrats: Fight the Villains, Defend the Dream (Feb 5, 2026)
**URL:** /polling/dem-message-test-2-5/
**Method:** MaxDiff analysis of 10 statements from prominent Democrats
**Sample:** 2,572 likely voters (Dec 5, 2025)

### Top Testing Messages (preference score):
- "Fights—really fights—for all of us" → +14 overall, +14 independents
- "Make the fight to restore the American Dream the heart of our party" → +12 overall, +13 independents
- "Laser-focus on making life affordable" + social safety net expansion → +17 (Democrats' 2nd most preferred)
- "Government that is every bit as ambitious as our adversaries" → +12 (Democrats)
- Calling out "oligarchy" → +3 Democrats, -4 independents, -6 overall

### Worst Testing Messages:
- "Performative woke politics" attack → -11 overall, -7 independents, -33 Democrats
- "Get rich" language with no named antagonist → -16 overall, -33 Democrats
